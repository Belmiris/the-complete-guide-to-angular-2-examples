72. Understanding the Component Life Cycle

  • ngOnChanges - Called after a bound input property changes (@Input). May be executed more than once.
  • ngOnInit - Called once the component is initialized. Runs after the constructor.
  • ngDoCheck - Called after every change in detection run. Runs a lot.
  • ngAfterContentInit - Called after the content [ng-content] has been projected into the view.
  • ngAfterContentChange - Called every time the projected content has been checked.
  • ngAfterViewInit - Called after the component's view (and child views) has been initialized.
  • ngAfterViewChecked - Called every time the view (and child views) have been checked.
  • ngOnDestroy - Called once the component is about to be destroyed.
